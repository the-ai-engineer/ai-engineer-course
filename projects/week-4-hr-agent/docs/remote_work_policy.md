# Acme Corporation Remote Work Policy

## Overview

Acme Corporation supports flexible work arrangements that allow employees to work remotely while maintaining productivity and collaboration. This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work.

## Eligibility

### Eligible Positions

Remote work may be available for positions that:

- Do not require physical presence (manufacturing, lab work, etc.)
- Can be performed effectively with available technology
- Have measurable deliverables
- Do not require constant in-person collaboration

### Employee Requirements

To be eligible for remote work, employees must:

- Have completed 90 days of employment
- Have satisfactory performance reviews
- Demonstrate self-motivation and time management skills
- Have reliable internet access and appropriate workspace
- Obtain manager approval

## Remote Work Options

### Fully Remote

Some positions are designated as fully remote, meaning employees work from home 100% of the time. These positions:

- Are posted as remote in job listings
- May require periodic travel for team meetings
- May have geographic restrictions based on tax and legal requirements

### Hybrid

Hybrid employees split time between office and remote work:

- Standard hybrid: 3 days office, 2 days remote
- Flexible hybrid: Agreed upon schedule with manager
- Office days should align with team collaboration needs

### Occasional Remote

Employees may work remotely occasionally with manager approval for:

- Personal appointments
- Focused work needs
- Inclement weather
- Other reasonable circumstances

## Work Hours and Availability

### Core Hours

Remote employees must be available during core business hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM in their local time zone. This ensures overlap for meetings and collaboration.

### Flexibility

Outside of core hours, employees may flex their schedule as long as they:

- Complete required work hours (typically 8 hours/day)
- Meet deadlines and deliverables
- Attend scheduled meetings
- Respond to urgent matters promptly

### Communication

Remote employees should:

- Keep calendar updated with availability
- Respond to messages within 4 hours during work hours
- Use video for meetings when possible
- Proactively communicate with team and manager

### Time Zone Considerations

For employees in different time zones than their team:

- Schedule meetings during overlapping hours when possible
- Rotate meeting times to share inconvenience fairly
- Use asynchronous communication when synchronous isn't necessary

## Workspace Requirements

### Home Office Setup

Remote employees must have:

- Dedicated workspace (doesn't have to be separate room)
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Quiet environment for calls and meetings
- Appropriate lighting and background for video calls
- Ergonomic setup (chair, desk, monitor at eye level)

### Equipment

**Company-Provided:**
- Laptop computer
- External monitor (if requested)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for calls
- Required software licenses

**Employee Responsibility:**
- Internet service
- Phone service (unless provided)
- Office furniture (reimbursement available per expense policy)
- Utilities

### Security Requirements

Home offices must meet security standards:

- Private, secure workspace (not public spaces for confidential work)
- Password-protected home WiFi (WPA2 or better)
- VPN usage for accessing company resources
- Screen privacy for confidential information
- Secure storage for any physical documents

## Expenses and Reimbursement

### Monthly Allowances

Remote employees receive:

- Internet reimbursement: $50/month
- Phone reimbursement: $50/month (if using personal phone for work)
- Office supplies: $25/month

### One-Time Equipment

New remote employees may be reimbursed up to $800 for home office setup:

- Desk and chair
- Monitor riser or standing desk converter
- Lighting
- Other ergonomic equipment

Submit receipts through normal expense process.

## Performance and Accountability

### Expectations

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as on-site employees. Managers will evaluate based on:

- Quality of work
- Meeting deadlines
- Collaboration and communication
- Responsiveness
- Achievement of goals

### Check-ins

Remote employees should have:

- Weekly 1:1 meetings with manager
- Regular team meetings
- Clear goals and metrics
- Access to performance feedback

### Underperformance

If remote work negatively impacts performance, employees may be required to:

- Increase office days
- Return to full-time office work
- Follow a performance improvement plan

## Collaboration and Team Building

### In-Person Requirements

Remote employees may be required to come to office for:

- Team planning sessions (quarterly)
- Company all-hands meetings
- Training sessions
- Important client meetings
- Team building events

Notice will be provided at least 2 weeks in advance when possible.

### Virtual Collaboration

Teams should use available tools to maintain connection:

- Video conferencing for meetings
- Instant messaging for quick questions
- Virtual coworking sessions
- Online social events

## Health and Safety

### Ergonomics

Employees are responsible for maintaining an ergonomic workspace. Resources available:

- Virtual ergonomic assessment
- Reimbursement for ergonomic equipment
- Guidelines on ergonomic.acmecorp.example.com

### Work-Life Balance

Remote employees should:

- Maintain regular work hours
- Take scheduled breaks
- Use PTO as normal
- Disconnect outside work hours
- Report any concerns about burnout or overwork

### Workers Compensation

Injuries occurring while working from home may be covered by workers compensation if they occur during work hours and are work-related. Report any injuries immediately to HR.

## Termination of Remote Work

Remote work arrangements may be terminated if:

- Business needs change
- Position requirements change
- Performance issues arise
- Employee violates policy
- Security concerns arise

Employees will receive 30 days notice before being required to return to office, when possible.

## Requesting Remote Work

To request a remote work arrangement:

1. Discuss with your manager
2. Submit request through HR portal
3. Specify type of arrangement requested
4. Provide justification
5. Await approval from manager and HR

Decisions are typically made within 2 weeks.
