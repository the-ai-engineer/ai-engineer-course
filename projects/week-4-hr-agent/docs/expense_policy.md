# Acme Corporation Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Overview

This policy establishes guidelines for business-related expenses incurred by employees. Acme Corporation will reimburse employees for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred while conducting company business.

## General Principles

### Approval Requirements

- Expenses under $100: No pre-approval required
- Expenses $100-$500: Manager approval required
- Expenses $500-$2,500: Director approval required
- Expenses over $2,500: VP approval required

### Documentation Requirements

All expense claims must include:

- Original receipt (itemized)
- Business purpose
- Names of attendees (for meals/entertainment)
- Date and location

Digital receipts are acceptable. Credit card statements alone are not sufficient documentation.

## Travel Expenses

### Air Travel

- Book economy class for domestic flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy allowed for international flights over 6 hours
- Business class requires VP approval
- Book flights at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use the company's preferred travel booking system

### Ground Transportation

**Rental Cars**
- Economy or intermediate class standard
- Full-size allowed when traveling with 2+ colleagues
- Insurance: Decline rental insurance (covered by corporate policy)
- GPS rental: Reimbursable
- Refueling: Required before return

**Rideshare/Taxi**
- Reimbursable for business travel
- Use when more economical than rental car
- Document destination and business purpose

**Personal Vehicle**
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.67 per mile (2024 IRS rate)
- Parking fees: Reimbursable
- Tolls: Reimbursable
- Document starting/ending locations

### Lodging

- Use company-preferred hotels when available
- Maximum nightly rate by city tier:
  - Tier 1 (NYC, SF, LA): $300/night
  - Tier 2 (Chicago, Boston, DC): $250/night
  - Tier 3 (Other major cities): $200/night
  - Tier 4 (Other areas): $150/night
- Exceptions require pre-approval from Finance

### Meals During Travel

Daily meal allowances for overnight business travel:

- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $50
- Total daily allowance: $95

Alcohol is not reimbursable except during approved client entertainment.

## Client Entertainment

### Meal Expenses

- Client meals require advance manager approval
- Maximum per person: $100 for dinner, $50 for lunch
- Document attendees, company represented, and business purpose
- Tips up to 20% are reimbursable

### Entertainment

- Events (concerts, sporting events, etc.) require director approval
- Maximum: $200 per person per event
- Must have clear business purpose
- Client attendance required

## Office and Supplies

### Office Supplies

- Routine supplies: Use office supply room or approved vendors
- Special items: Manager approval required
- Use company credit card when available

### Technology

- Software purchases require IT approval
- Hardware purchases require IT and manager approval
- Cloud services require IT security review

### Home Office Equipment

Remote employees may be reimbursed for:

- Desk and chair: Up to $500 (one-time)
- Monitor: Up to $300 (one-time)
- Keyboard and mouse: Up to $100 (one-time)
- Internet: $50/month
- Office supplies: $25/month

Receipts required for all home office expenses.

## Professional Development

### Conferences and Training

- Registration fees: Pre-approval required
- Travel: Follow standard travel policy
- Maximum per event: $2,500 (including travel)
- Limit: 2 conferences per year

### Certifications

- Exam fees: Reimbursed upon passing
- Study materials: Up to $500 per certification
- Renewal fees: Reimbursed for job-relevant certifications

### Books and Subscriptions

- Professional books: Up to $200/year
- Online subscriptions: Require manager approval

## Expense Submission

### Process

1. Submit expenses through the expense portal (expenses.acmecorp.example.com)
2. Upload receipts for all expenses
3. Include required details and business justification
4. Submit within 30 days of incurring expense
5. Manager approves within 5 business days
6. Reimbursement processed within 10 business days of approval

### Late Submissions

Expenses submitted more than 60 days after being incurred may be denied. Exceptions require VP approval.

### Corporate Credit Card

Employees issued a corporate credit card must:

- Use only for business expenses
- Submit expense reports monthly
- Pay personal charges immediately
- Report lost/stolen cards within 24 hours

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursable:

- Personal expenses
- Alcohol (except approved client entertainment)
- Entertainment for yourself
- First-class airfare (without approval)
- Luxury hotels (without approval)
- Gym memberships (see wellness program for allowance)
- Clothing (unless required uniform)
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Pet care during travel
- Personal phone bills (unless approved for reimbursement)
- Expenses for family members

## Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Denial of reimbursement
- Requirement to repay inappropriate expenses
- Revocation of corporate credit card
- Disciplinary action

Suspected fraud will be investigated and may result in termination.
