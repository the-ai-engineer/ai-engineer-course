# Acme Corporation Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Acme Corporation

Welcome to Acme Corporation! We are excited to have you join our team. This handbook provides an overview of our company policies, procedures, and expectations.

## Company Values

At Acme Corporation, we are guided by the following core values:

- **Integrity**: We act honestly and ethically in all our business dealings.
- **Innovation**: We encourage creative thinking and continuous improvement.
- **Collaboration**: We work together as a team to achieve our goals.
- **Excellence**: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do.
- **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity and respect.

## Employment Relationship

### At-Will Employment

Employment at Acme Corporation is at-will unless otherwise specified in a written employment contract. This means that either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice.

### Equal Opportunity Employment

Acme Corporation is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected characteristic.

## Work Hours and Schedules

### Standard Work Hours

The standard work week at Acme Corporation is 40 hours, typically Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Some positions may have different schedules based on business needs.

### Overtime

Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5 times their regular hourly rate for hours worked over 40 in a work week. Overtime must be approved by your manager in advance.

### Time Tracking

All employees are required to accurately record their work hours using the company's time tracking system. Time records must be submitted by the end of each pay period.

## Workplace Conduct

### Professional Standards

All employees are expected to maintain professional standards of conduct. This includes:

- Treating colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
- Maintaining a positive and cooperative attitude
- Following company policies and procedures
- Protecting confidential information
- Avoiding conflicts of interest

### Dress Code

Acme Corporation maintains a business casual dress code. Employees should dress in a neat, clean, and professional manner appropriate for their role and work environment.

### Harassment and Discrimination

Acme Corporation has zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination of any kind. All employees are expected to contribute to a respectful and inclusive workplace. Any incidents should be reported to HR immediately.

## Performance Management

### Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted annually. During these reviews, managers and employees discuss:

- Goals and objectives
- Achievements and areas for improvement
- Career development opportunities
- Compensation adjustments (when applicable)

### Feedback and Communication

We encourage ongoing feedback and open communication between employees and managers. Regular one-on-one meetings are recommended to discuss progress and address any concerns.

## Separation from Employment

### Resignation

Employees wishing to resign should provide at least two weeks written notice to their manager. This allows for a smooth transition of responsibilities.

### Exit Interviews

All departing employees are invited to participate in an exit interview with HR. This provides an opportunity to share feedback about your experience at Acme Corporation.
