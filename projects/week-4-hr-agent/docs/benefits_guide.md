# Acme Corporation Benefits Guide

## Overview

Acme Corporation offers a comprehensive benefits package to support the health, well-being, and financial security of our employees and their families. This guide provides an overview of all available benefits.

## Eligibility

### Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees (working 30+ hours per week) are eligible for all benefits beginning on the first day of the month following their start date.

### Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees (working 20-29 hours per week) are eligible for limited benefits including 401(k) participation and employee assistance program access.

## Health Insurance

### Medical Plans

Acme Corporation offers three medical plan options:

**PPO Plan**
- Monthly premium: $150/employee, $400/family
- Deductible: $500/individual, $1,000/family
- Copay: $25 for primary care, $50 for specialists
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $4,000/individual, $8,000/family

**High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)**
- Monthly premium: $75/employee, $200/family
- Deductible: $1,500/individual, $3,000/family
- Eligible for Health Savings Account (HSA)
- Company contributes $500/year to HSA

**HMO Plan**
- Monthly premium: $125/employee, $350/family
- Deductible: None
- Copay: $20 for all visits
- Requires referrals for specialists

### Dental Insurance

We offer two dental plans:

**Basic Plan**
- Monthly premium: $20/employee, $50/family
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic procedures: 80% covered
- Major procedures: 50% covered
- Annual maximum: $1,500

**Premium Plan**
- Monthly premium: $35/employee, $85/family
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic procedures: 90% covered
- Major procedures: 70% covered
- Orthodontia: 50% covered (lifetime max $2,500)
- Annual maximum: $2,500

### Vision Insurance

- Monthly premium: $10/employee, $25/family
- Eye exam: $10 copay, once per year
- Lenses: $25 copay
- Frames: $150 allowance every two years
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance in lieu of frames

## Retirement Benefits

### 401(k) Plan

Acme Corporation offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan with the following features:

- **Employee contributions**: Up to IRS annual limit ($23,000 in 2024)
- **Company match**: 100% match on first 3%, plus 50% match on next 2% (total match up to 4%)
- **Vesting schedule**: Immediate vesting on employee contributions, 3-year cliff vesting on company match
- **Investment options**: Over 20 investment funds including target-date funds
- **Roth option**: Available for after-tax contributions

### Eligibility

Employees are eligible to participate in the 401(k) plan after 30 days of employment. Company matching begins after 6 months of service.

## Life and Disability Insurance

### Life Insurance

- **Basic life**: 1x annual salary, paid by company (up to $250,000)
- **Supplemental life**: Available up to 5x annual salary at employee expense
- **Spouse life**: Available up to $100,000
- **Child life**: Available up to $10,000 per child

### Disability Insurance

**Short-Term Disability**
- Covers 60% of salary
- Begins after 7-day waiting period
- Maximum duration: 12 weeks
- Paid by company

**Long-Term Disability**
- Covers 60% of salary
- Begins after short-term disability exhausted
- Maximum duration: Until age 65
- Paid by company

## Additional Benefits

### Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Free confidential counseling and support services for employees and family members:

- Mental health counseling (up to 6 sessions per year)
- Legal consultation
- Financial planning advice
- Work-life resources
- Crisis support (24/7 hotline)

### Wellness Program

- Annual fitness reimbursement: Up to $500/year
- On-site gym access at headquarters
- Wellness challenges and incentives
- Annual health screenings
- Flu shots (free on-site)

### Professional Development

- Tuition reimbursement: Up to $5,250/year
- Conference attendance budget
- LinkedIn Learning access
- Internal training programs
- Certification exam reimbursement

### Commuter Benefits

- Pre-tax transit benefits up to IRS limit
- Pre-tax parking benefits up to IRS limit
- Bike commuter reimbursement: $50/month

## Enrollment

### Open Enrollment

Open enrollment occurs annually in November for coverage beginning January 1. During open enrollment, you can:

- Enroll in new benefits
- Change your current elections
- Add or remove dependents

### Qualifying Life Events

Outside of open enrollment, you may change benefits within 30 days of a qualifying life event:

- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
