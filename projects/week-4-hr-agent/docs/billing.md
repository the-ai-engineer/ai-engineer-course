# Billing and Pricing

Zen HR offers flexible pricing plans to fit organizations of all sizes.

## Plans Overview

### Starter Plan - $6 per employee per month

Best for small teams getting started with HR software.

**Includes:**
- Up to 25 employees
- Employee directory and profiles
- Time-off management
- Basic reporting
- Document storage (5GB)
- Email support (response within 24 hours)
- Mobile app access

**Does not include:**
- Performance reviews
- Custom workflows
- API access
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support

### Professional Plan - $12 per employee per month

Best for growing companies that need advanced features.

**Includes everything in Starter, plus:**
- Unlimited employees
- Performance review cycles
- Custom workflows and automations
- API access
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Document storage (50GB)
- Priority support (response within 4 hours)
- SSO integration
- Custom fields

### Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing

Best for large organizations with complex needs.

**Includes everything in Professional, plus:**
- Dedicated customer success manager
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Custom integrations
- On-premise deployment option
- Unlimited document storage
- Phone support
- Custom training sessions
- Security review and compliance documentation
- Volume discounts

Contact sales@zenhr.com for Enterprise pricing.

## Billing Cycles

### Monthly Billing

- Charged on the same date each month
- Pay only for active employees
- Changes reflected in the next billing cycle
- Cancel anytime

### Annual Billing

- **Save 20%** compared to monthly billing
- Pay for 10 months, get 12 months of service
- Billed as a single annual payment
- Changes prorated over remaining months

To switch to annual billing:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click "Switch to Annual"
3. Review the prorated amount
4. Confirm payment

## Employee Count and Billing

### How Employee Count Works

Your bill is based on the number of **active employees** at the time of billing:

- New employees added mid-cycle: Prorated from their start date
- Employees deactivated mid-cycle: Prorated to their deactivation date
- Contractors and part-time employees count as full employees

### Viewing Your Employee Count

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. View "Current Employee Count"
3. See breakdown by status (active, pending, deactivated)

## Payment Methods

### Credit Card

Accepted cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

To add or update your card:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment Methods**
2. Click "Add Card" or "Update"
3. Enter card details
4. Set as default payment method

### ACH Bank Transfer

Available for Enterprise customers:

1. Contact your account manager
2. Provide bank account details
3. Complete verification (2-3 business days)
4. Future invoices paid via ACH

### Invoice Payment

Enterprise customers can pay via invoice:

- Net 30 payment terms
- Invoices sent at the start of each billing period
- Pay by check, wire transfer, or ACH

## Invoices and Receipts

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoices**
2. View all past invoices
3. Download PDF for any invoice
4. Filter by date range

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:
- Billing period
- Employee count
- Plan and pricing
- Taxes (if applicable)
- Payment method used

### Adding Billing Information

To add company details to invoices:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Company Info**
2. Enter company name and address
3. Add tax ID (optional)
4. Save changes

## Upgrading Your Plan

### From Starter to Professional

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Review new features and pricing
4. Confirm upgrade

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle.

### From Professional to Enterprise

1. Contact sales@zenhr.com
2. Discuss your requirements
3. Receive a custom quote
4. Complete enterprise agreement

## Downgrading Your Plan

### From Professional to Starter

Before downgrading, ensure:
- You have 25 or fewer employees
- You've exported any data from Professional-only features

To downgrade:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select Starter
4. Acknowledge feature limitations
5. Confirm downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

## Cancellation

### Canceling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Complete the exit survey (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

After cancellation:
- Access continues until the end of your billing period
- Data is retained for 30 days
- You can reactivate within 30 days without data loss
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted

### Requesting a Refund

Refunds are available for:
- Annual plans within 14 days of payment
- Billing errors
- Duplicate charges

Contact billing@zenhr.com for refund requests.

## Taxes

### Sales Tax

Zen HR collects sales tax where required by law. Tax is calculated based on your company's billing address.

### Tax Exemption

If your organization is tax-exempt:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Tax Info**
2. Upload your tax exemption certificate
3. Allow 2-3 business days for processing
4. Future invoices will exclude tax

## Common Billing Questions

**When am I charged for new employees?**
New employees are prorated from their start date to your next billing date.

**What happens if my payment fails?**
We'll retry the payment after 3 days. If it fails again, we'll send an email notification. After 14 days of failed payments, your account may be suspended.

**Can I get a discount for nonprofits?**
Yes! Nonprofits receive 25% off any plan. Contact support@zenhr.com with proof of nonprofit status.

**Do you offer discounts for startups?**
Startups with fewer than 10 employees can apply for our Startup Program, which includes 50% off for the first year. Apply at zenhr.com/startups.
