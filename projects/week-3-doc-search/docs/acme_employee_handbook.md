## Welcome to AcmeCorp

Welcome to AcmeCorp! We're excited to have you join our team. This handbook outlines our company policies, benefits, and expectations. Please read it carefully and reach out to HR with any questions.

AcmeCorp was founded in 2010 and has grown to over 500 employees across our headquarters in Austin, Texas and regional offices in Denver, Chicago, and New York. Our mission is to build innovative software solutions that help businesses thrive.

## Section 1: Benefits Overview

### 1.1 Health Insurance

AcmeCorp offers comprehensive health insurance coverage to all full-time employees starting on the first day of the month following your hire date.

**Medical Plans Available:**

**Gold PPO Plan**
- Monthly Premium: $150 (employee only), $400 (employee + family)
- Deductible: $500 individual, $1,000 family
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $3,000 individual, $6,000 family
- Primary Care Visit: $25 copay
- Specialist Visit: $50 copay
- Emergency Room: $250 copay
- Prescription Drugs: $10 generic, $35 brand, $70 specialty

**Silver HDHP Plan**
- Monthly Premium: $75 (employee only), $200 (employee + family)
- Deductible: $1,500 individual, $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $5,000 individual, $10,000 family
- HSA Eligible: Yes
- AcmeCorp HSA Contribution: $500 annually for individual, $1,000 for family

### 1.2 Dental Insurance

Dental coverage is provided through Delta Dental at no cost to employees for basic coverage.

**Coverage Details:**
- Preventive Care: 100% covered (cleanings, x-rays, exams)
- Basic Procedures: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major Procedures: 50% covered (crowns, bridges, dentures)
- Orthodontia: 50% covered up to $2,000 lifetime maximum (dependents under 19 only)
- Annual Maximum Benefit: $2,500 per person

### 1.3 Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is provided through VSP Vision Care.

**Coverage Details:**
- Eye Exam: $10 copay, once per year
- Prescription Glasses: $25 copay, $150 allowance for frames
- Contact Lenses: $150 allowance in lieu of glasses
- Lens Enhancements: 20% discount on progressive lenses, anti-reflective coating

### 1.4 Life Insurance

AcmeCorp provides basic life insurance at no cost to employees.

**Basic Coverage:**
- Coverage Amount: 2x annual base salary (up to $500,000 maximum)
- Accidental Death & Dismemberment: Equal to life insurance amount

**Supplemental Coverage:**
- Employees may purchase additional coverage up to 5x annual salary
- Spouse coverage available up to $100,000
- Child coverage available at $10,000 per child
- Evidence of Insurability required for amounts over $200,000

### 1.5 Disability Insurance

**Short-Term Disability:**
- Benefit: 60% of weekly salary
- Maximum Weekly Benefit: $2,500
- Waiting Period: 7 days for illness, 0 days for injury
- Maximum Duration: 12 weeks

**Long-Term Disability:**
- Benefit: 60% of monthly salary
- Maximum Monthly Benefit: $10,000
- Waiting Period: 90 days
- Maximum Duration: Until age 65 or Social Security Normal Retirement Age

### 1.6 401(k) Retirement Plan

AcmeCorp offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan through Fidelity Investments.

**Plan Details:**
- Eligibility: All employees age 21+ after 30 days of employment
- Employee Contribution Limit: $23,000 per year (2025 IRS limit)
- Catch-up Contributions: Additional $7,500 for employees age 50+

**Company Match:**
- AcmeCorp matches 100% of the first 4% of salary you contribute
- AcmeCorp matches 50% of the next 2% of salary you contribute
- Maximum company match: 5% of your annual salary

**Vesting Schedule:**
- Employee contributions: 100% immediately vested
- Company match: 3-year graded vesting (33% per year)
- After 3 years: 100% vested in company match

**Investment Options:**
- Target Date Funds
- Index Funds (S&P 500, Total Stock Market, International)
- Bond Funds
- Company Stock (limited to 10% of portfolio)

### 1.7 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

Eligible employees may purchase AcmeCorp stock at a 15% discount through payroll deductions.

- Contribution Limit: 1% to 15% of base salary
- Purchase Periods: Every 6 months (January and July)
- Discount: 15% off the lower of the price at the beginning or end of the purchase period

### 1.8 Flexible Spending Accounts

**Healthcare FSA:**
- Annual Contribution Limit: $3,200
- Eligible Expenses: Medical, dental, vision expenses not covered by insurance
- Rollover: Up to $640 rolls over to the next year

**Dependent Care FSA:**
- Annual Contribution Limit: $5,000
- Eligible Expenses: Daycare, preschool, elder care, summer camps

## Section 2: Paid Time Off

### 2.1 Vacation Days

AcmeCorp provides paid vacation days based on your length of service with the company.

**Vacation Accrual Schedule:**
- 0 to 1 year of service: 10 days per year (accrues at 3.33 hours per pay period)
- 1 to 3 years of service: 15 days per year (accrues at 5.0 hours per pay period)
- 3 to 5 years of service: 20 days per year (accrues at 6.67 hours per pay period)
- 5 to 10 years of service: 25 days per year (accrues at 8.33 hours per pay period)
- 10+ years of service: 30 days per year (accrues at 10.0 hours per pay period)

**Vacation Policy Rules:**
- Vacation time begins accruing on your first day of employment
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Requests for more than 5 consecutive days require manager approval 30 days in advance
- Maximum vacation balance: 1.5x your annual accrual rate
- Upon termination, accrued unused vacation will be paid out at your final rate of pay

### 2.2 Personal Days

All full-time employees receive 3 personal days per calendar year.

- Personal days do not roll over to the next year
- Personal days are not paid out upon termination
- Personal days can be used for any reason without explanation
- 24-hour advance notice is appreciated but not required

### 2.3 Floating Holidays

Employees receive 2 floating holidays per year to use at their discretion.

- Floating holidays can be used for religious observances, cultural celebrations, or personal occasions
- Floating holidays do not roll over and are not paid out upon termination
- Request floating holidays at least 1 week in advance

## Section 4: Sick Leave

### 4.1 Sick Leave Policy

AcmeCorp provides paid sick leave to all employees for illness, injury, or medical appointments.

**Sick Leave Accrual:**
- Full-time employees: 1 day (8 hours) per month, up to 12 days per year
- Part-time employees: Prorated based on hours worked

**Sick Leave Balance:**
- Maximum accrual: 60 days (480 hours)
- Unused sick leave rolls over year to year
- Sick leave is not paid out upon termination

### 4.2 Acceptable Uses of Sick Leave

Sick leave may be used for:
- Personal illness or injury
- Medical, dental, or vision appointments
- Care for an immediate family member who is ill
- Mental health days
- Quarantine or isolation due to contagious illness

### 4.3 Reporting Sick Leave

- Notify your manager as soon as possible, preferably before your scheduled start time
- For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required
- Extended illness may qualify for Short-Term Disability coverage

### 4.4 Sick Leave Donation Program

Employees may donate unused sick leave to colleagues experiencing serious illness or family emergencies. Contact HR to participate in the Sick Leave Donation Program.

## Section 5: Parental Leave

### 5.1 Maternity Leave

Birth mothers are eligible for up to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave at 100% of base salary.

- Leave may begin up to 2 weeks before the expected due date
- Additional unpaid leave may be available under FMLA
- Benefits continue during maternity leave

### 5.2 Paternity and Parental Leave

Non-birth parents, including fathers, adoptive parents, and foster parents, are eligible for 8 weeks of paid parental leave at 100% of base salary.

- Leave must be taken within 12 months of birth, adoption, or foster placement
- Leave may be taken consecutively or in two separate blocks with manager approval

### 5.3 Adoption and Foster Care Assistance

AcmeCorp provides financial assistance for adoption and foster care:
- Adoption Reimbursement: Up to $10,000 for adoption-related expenses
- Foster Care Support: Up to $2,500 per year for foster care expenses

## Section 6: Additional Leave Types

### 6.1 Bereavement Leave

Employees may take paid bereavement leave for the death of a family member:
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days
- Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law, aunt, uncle): 3 days
- Close friend or colleague: 1 day

### 6.2 Jury Duty

Employees summoned for jury duty will receive full pay for up to 10 days. If jury service extends beyond 10 days, contact HR to discuss extended leave arrangements.

### 6.3 Military Leave

Employees who are members of the military reserves or National Guard are entitled to unpaid leave for training and active duty as required by law. AcmeCorp will make up the difference between military pay and regular pay for up to 12 months.

### 6.4 Voting Leave

Employees may take up to 2 hours of paid time off to vote in federal, state, or local elections if their work schedule does not allow sufficient time outside of working hours.

## Section 7: Remote Work Policy

### 7.1 Hybrid Work Schedule

AcmeCorp offers a hybrid work arrangement for most positions:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- In-office days are typically Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday
- Specific schedules should be coordinated with your manager and team

### 7.2 Fully Remote Positions

Some positions are approved for fully remote work. Fully remote employees must:
- Maintain a dedicated home office space
- Be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM in their local time zone)
- Attend in-person meetings quarterly (travel expenses covered)

### 7.3 Home Office Stipend

Remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time $500 home office stipend for equipment and supplies. Receipts must be submitted within 90 days of your start date.
