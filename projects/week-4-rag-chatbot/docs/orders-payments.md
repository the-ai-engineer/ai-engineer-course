# Orders & Payments

## Placing an Order

### How to Order

1. Browse products at acmestore.com
2. Select size, color, and quantity
3. Click "Add to Cart"
4. When ready, click the cart icon and select "Checkout"
5. Enter shipping address
6. Select shipping method
7. Enter payment information
8. Review and place order

### Guest Checkout

You can checkout without creating an account. You'll receive order updates via email. To track your order or make returns, use your order number and email address.

### Order Confirmation

After placing an order, you'll receive:
- **Immediately**: Order confirmation email with order number
- **Within 24-48 hours**: Shipping confirmation with tracking number

Didn't receive confirmation? Check your spam folder or contact us with your payment details.

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payment Types

- **Credit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Debit Cards**: Any card with Visa/Mastercard logo
- **PayPal**: Including Pay in 4
- **Apple Pay**: On Safari and iOS devices
- **Google Pay**: On Chrome and Android devices
- **Acme Gift Cards**: Enter code at checkout
- **Acme Store Credit**: Automatically applied if available
- **Klarna**: Buy now, pay later in 4 installments
- **Afterpay**: Pay in 4 interest-free installments

### Buy Now, Pay Later

**Klarna** and **Afterpay** let you split your purchase into 4 interest-free payments:
- Available on orders $35-$1,000
- First payment due at checkout
- Remaining 3 payments every 2 weeks
- No interest if paid on time
- Approval based on Klarna/Afterpay credit check

### Payment Security

All transactions are secured with:
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- PCI DSS compliance
- Fraud detection monitoring
- 3D Secure verification for eligible cards

We never store your full credit card number.

## Order Issues

### Cancel an Order

Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placement:

1. Log into your account
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the order
4. Click "Cancel Order" (if available)

After 1 hour, orders enter processing and cannot be canceled. You'll need to wait for delivery and then return the item.

### Modify an Order

We cannot modify orders after placement. This includes:
- Changing items
- Changing sizes/colors
- Changing quantities
- Updating shipping address
- Changing shipping method

If you need changes, cancel the order (within 1 hour) and place a new one.

### Order Not Received

If your order hasn't arrived by the estimated delivery date:

1. Check tracking status at acmestore.com/track
2. If tracking shows "Delivered" - see our Shipping Issues guide
3. If tracking shows "In Transit" past delivery date - wait 2 more business days
4. Still no package? Contact us for investigation or replacement

### Charged but No Confirmation

If you were charged but didn't receive order confirmation:

1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Search email for "acme" or the order total amount
3. Log into your account and check Order History
4. Contact us with last 4 digits of payment card and amount charged

We can locate your order and resend confirmation.

## Promo Codes & Discounts

### How to Apply a Promo Code

1. Add items to cart
2. Click cart icon to view cart
3. Enter code in "Promo Code" field
4. Click "Apply"
5. Discount will show in order summary

### Promo Code Rules

- One promo code per order
- Cannot be combined with other offers (unless stated)
- Some exclusions may apply (premium brands, sale items)
- Codes are case-insensitive
- Expired codes will not work

### Common Promo Code Issues

**"Code Invalid"**
- Check for typos
- Code may have expired
- Code may have usage limits reached

**"Code Not Applicable"**
- Your cart may not meet minimum spend
- Items in cart may be excluded
- Code may be for first-time customers only

### Student & Military Discounts

- **Students**: 15% off with valid .edu email verification via SheerID
- **Military**: 15% off with military ID verification via ID.me
- Cannot be combined with other offers
- Register at acmestore.com/discounts

## Billing Issues

### Double Charged

If you see duplicate charges:

1. Check if one is a "pending" authorization (these drop off in 3-5 days)
2. Verify you didn't accidentally place two orders
3. If truly double-charged, contact us with both transaction details
4. We'll refund the duplicate within 5-7 business days

### Unexpected Charge

An unexpected charge from ACME STORE may be:
- A pre-order that shipped
- A backordered item now shipped
- A subscription renewal
- A previous order finally processing

Log into your account to view all orders, or contact us with the charge amount and date.

### Payment Declined

If your payment was declined:

- Verify card details are correct
- Check available balance/credit limit
- Call your bank - they may be blocking the transaction
- Try a different payment method
- Clear browser cache and try again
