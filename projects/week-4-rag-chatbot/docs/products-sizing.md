### How to Measure

**Bust/Chest**: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level.

**Waist**: Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.

**Hips**: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.

**Inseam**: Measure from the crotch to the bottom of the leg.

**Foot Length**: Stand on paper, trace your foot, and measure from heel to longest toe.

## Product Care

### Washing Instructions

**Machine Wash Cold**
- Use cold water (30°C/86°F or below)
- Gentle or delicate cycle
- Turn inside out for prints and dark colors
- Wash similar colors together

**Hand Wash**
- Fill basin with cool water
- Add mild detergent
- Gently agitate
- Rinse thoroughly
- Do not wring

**Dry Clean Only**
- Take to professional cleaner
- Point out any stains
- Includes silk, wool, and structured items

### Drying Instructions

**Tumble Dry Low**
- Use low heat setting
- Remove promptly to prevent wrinkles
- Do not over-dry

**Line Dry**
- Hang or lay flat
- Away from direct sunlight
- Reshape while damp

**Do Not Tumble Dry**
- Air dry only
- Includes delicate fabrics and items prone to shrinking

### Fabric Care Tips

**Cotton**: Wash cold, tumble dry low. May shrink slightly on first wash.

**Denim**: Wash inside out in cold water. Wash infrequently to preserve color and fit.

**Wool**: Hand wash or dry clean. Lay flat to dry. Do not hang wet wool.

**Silk**: Dry clean recommended. If hand washing, use cool water and silk detergent.

**Synthetic (Polyester, Nylon)**: Machine wash cold. Tumble dry low or hang dry.

**Linen**: Machine wash cold. Iron while slightly damp for best results.
