# Returns & Refunds

## Return Policy Overview

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

### Return Eligibility

**Eligible for Return:**
- Items in original, unused condition
- Items with all tags attached
- Items in original packaging
- Items returned within 30 days of delivery

**Not Eligible for Return:**
- Final sale items (marked on product page)
- Personalized or custom items
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Gift cards
- Items damaged through normal wear
- Items without original tags

### Extended Holiday Returns

Orders placed between November 1st and December 31st can be returned until January 31st of the following year.

## How to Start a Return

### Online Returns

1. Log into your account at acmestore.com
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the order containing the item(s) to return
4. Click "Start Return" and select your items
5. Choose your reason for return
6. Select refund method (original payment or store credit)
7. Print your prepaid shipping label
8. Pack items securely and drop off at any FedEx location

### In-Store Returns

Bring your items and order confirmation to any Acme Store location. Store associates can process your return immediately. Find your nearest store at acmestore.com/stores.

### Return Without Account

If you checked out as a guest:
1. Visit acmestore.com/returns
2. Enter your order number and email address
3. Follow the return prompts

## Refund Information

### Refund Timeline

- **Store Credit**: Issued within 24 hours of return receipt
- **Credit Card**: 5-7 business days after return is processed
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days after return is processed
- **Debit Card**: 5-10 business days after return is processed

### Refund Amount

Your refund will include:
- Full item price
- Original taxes paid

Your refund will NOT include:
- Original shipping charges (unless item was defective or we made an error)
- Return shipping charges (unless item was defective or we made an error)

### Partial Refunds

We may issue a partial refund for:
- Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days (75% refund)
- Items with missing tags (85% refund)
- Items showing signs of use (case-by-case basis)

## Exchanges

### How to Exchange

We don't offer direct exchanges. To get a different size or color:

1. Return your original item for a refund
2. Place a new order for the item you want

**Tip**: Choose store credit for faster processing - it's issued within 24 hours and can be used immediately.

### Price Adjustments

If an item you purchased goes on sale within 14 days of your purchase, contact us for a price adjustment. We'll refund the difference to your original payment method.

## Defective or Incorrect Items

### Defective Items

If you received a defective item:

1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery
2. Provide photos of the defect
3. We'll send a prepaid return label
4. Choose: replacement, refund, or store credit
5. Full refund including original shipping

### Wrong Item Received

If we sent the wrong item:

1. Contact us immediately
2. Do not discard the incorrect item
3. We'll send a prepaid return label and ship the correct item
4. Keep the wrong item? We may offer a discount to keep it

## Return Shipping

### Free Return Shipping

Return shipping is free when:
- Item is defective
- We sent the wrong item
- You're a Premium Member

### Paid Return Shipping

A $6.99 return shipping fee is deducted from your refund for:
- Change of mind returns
- Size/fit issues
- Style preference changes

### International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Refunds will be issued minus a $15 international processing fee.

## Gift Returns

If you received a gift and need to return it:

1. Visit acmestore.com/gift-returns
2. Enter the order number from the packing slip
3. You'll receive store credit for the item value
4. The original purchaser will NOT be notified

Don't have the order number? Contact us with a description of the item and we'll help locate the order.
