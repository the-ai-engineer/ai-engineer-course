## Frequently Asked Questions

### Orders

**Where is my order?**
Track your order at acmestore.com/track with your order number and email address. You'll see real-time tracking updates once your order ships.

**Can I change my order?**
Orders cannot be modified after placement. If you need changes, cancel within 1 hour and place a new order.

**Why was my order canceled?**
Orders may be canceled due to: payment issues, suspected fraud, item unavailability, or shipping address problems. You'll receive an email explaining the reason.

### Payments

**When will I be charged?**
Your card is charged when your order ships. For pre-orders, you're charged immediately but can cancel anytime before shipping.

**Can I use multiple payment methods?**
Yes, you can combine a gift card or store credit with another payment method. However, you cannot split payment across multiple credit cards.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
Yes, we offer Klarna and Afterpay for purchases $35-$1,000. Split into 4 interest-free payments.

### Shipping

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes, we ship to over 100 countries. International shipping starts at $19.99. Customs duties and taxes are the recipient's responsibility.

**Can I change my shipping address?**
Before shipping: Contact us immediately. After shipping: We cannot redirect packages in transit.

**What if my package is stolen?**
Contact us within 7 days of delivery with a police report (if filed). We'll investigate and may provide a one-time replacement.

### Returns

**How do I return an item?**
Log into your account, go to Order History, select the order, and click "Start Return." Print the prepaid label and drop off at any FedEx location.

**Do I need the receipt to return?**
No, we can look up your order using your email address or the credit card used.

**Can I return a gift?**
Yes, gift returns receive store credit. Visit acmestore.com/gift-returns with the order number from the packing slip.

### Account

**I forgot my password**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

**How do I close my account?**
Go to Account Settings and click "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page. This cannot be undone.

**Why can't I log in?**
Common issues: wrong email address, caps lock on, need password reset, or account was deleted. Try "Forgot Password" or contact support.

## Report a Problem

### Website Issues

Report bugs or issues:
- Email: tech@acmestore.com
- Include screenshots and browser/device information
- Describe steps to reproduce the issue

### Product Safety

Report product safety concerns:
- Email: safety@acmestore.com
- Call: 1-800-ACME-SHOP and ask for Safety Team
- Include product name, order number, and detailed description

### Fraud or Scams

Report suspected fraud:
- Forward suspicious emails to phishing@acmestore.com
- Never click links in emails asking for personal information
- We will never ask for your password via email
