# Account & Membership

## Creating an Account

### Why Create an Account?

Benefits of having an Acme account:
- Faster checkout with saved addresses and payment methods
- Order tracking and history
- Easy returns and exchanges
- Wishlist to save items for later
- Exclusive member-only offers
- Earn and redeem rewards points

### How to Sign Up

1. Visit acmestore.com and click "Sign In"
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Fill in your name
5. Optional: Add phone number for order updates via SMS
6. Click "Create Account"

You'll receive a welcome email with a 15% off code for your first order.

## Managing Your Account

### Update Personal Information

1. Log into your account
2. Go to Account Settings
3. Click "Edit" next to the section you want to update
4. Make changes and click "Save"

You can update:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password
- Communication preferences

### Saved Addresses

Add multiple shipping addresses for faster checkout:

1. Go to Account Settings > Addresses
2. Click "Add New Address"
3. Enter address details
4. Optional: Set as default shipping address
5. Click "Save Address"

You can save up to 10 addresses.

### Saved Payment Methods

Save cards for faster checkout:

1. Go to Account Settings > Payment Methods
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter card details
4. Click "Save"

Your card information is securely stored and tokenized - we never see your full card number.

### Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to Account Settings
2. Scroll to bottom and click "Delete Account"
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click "Permanently Delete"

Account deletion will:
- Remove all personal information
- Cancel any active subscriptions
- Forfeit unredeemed rewards points and store credit
- This action cannot be undone

Order history is retained for legal/tax purposes but anonymized.

## Password & Security

### Reset Forgotten Password

1. Go to acmestore.com and click "Sign In"
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check email for reset link (valid for 24 hours)
5. Click link and create new password

### Password Requirements

Your password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number

### Account Security Tips

- Use a unique password not used on other sites
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out on shared devices
- Contact us immediately if you notice unauthorized activity

## Acme Rewards Program

### How It Works

Earn points on every purchase and redeem for discounts.

**Earning Points:**
- 1 point per $1 spent
- Bonus points on special promotions
- 100 points for writing a product review
- 50 points on your birthday

**Redeeming Points:**
- 100 points = $5 off
- 200 points = $10 off
- 500 points = $30 off
- 1000 points = $75 off

### Rewards Tiers

**Member** (0-499 points/year)
- Earn 1 point per $1
- Birthday bonus points
- Member-only sales access

**Silver** (500-999 points/year)
- Earn 1.25 points per $1
- Free standard shipping
- Early access to new arrivals

**Gold** (1000+ points/year)
- Earn 1.5 points per $1
- Free express shipping
- Exclusive Gold member events
- Free gift wrapping
- Priority customer service

### Points Expiration

Points expire 12 months after they're earned. Check your points balance at Account > Rewards.

## Acme Premium Membership

### Premium Benefits

For $99/year, Premium members get:

- **Free unlimited shipping** on all orders (no minimum)
- **Free returns** on all items
- **Early access** to sales (24 hours before public)
- **Exclusive discounts** throughout the year
- **Double points** on all purchases
- **Free gift wrapping** on all orders
- **Priority customer service** (skip the queue)

### Join Premium

1. Go to acmestore.com/premium
2. Click "Join Premium"
3. Enter payment information
4. Membership activates immediately

### Cancel Premium

You can cancel anytime:

1. Go to Account Settings > Premium Membership
2. Click "Cancel Membership"
3. Confirm cancellation

You'll keep benefits until your membership period ends. We offer prorated refunds if you cancel within the first 30 days.

## Communication Preferences

### Email Preferences

Manage what emails you receive:

1. Go to Account Settings > Communication
2. Toggle each email type on/off:
   - Order updates (cannot be disabled)
   - Shipping notifications (cannot be disabled)
   - Promotional emails
   - New arrivals
   - Back-in-stock alerts
   - Rewards updates
   - Product recommendations

### Unsubscribe

Click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any promotional email, or adjust settings in your account. Order and shipping emails cannot be disabled.

### SMS Updates

Opt in to receive text updates:
- Order confirmation
- Shipping updates
- Delivery notifications

Standard message rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out anytime.
