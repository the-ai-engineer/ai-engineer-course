# Shipping & Delivery

## Shipping Methods

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost**: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Carrier**: USPS or FedEx Ground

### Express Shipping
- **Cost**: $12.99
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Carrier**: FedEx Express

### Next-Day Shipping
- **Cost**: $24.99
- **Delivery Time**: 1 business day (order by 2 PM EST)
- **Carrier**: FedEx Priority Overnight
- **Availability**: Not available for PO boxes or Alaska/Hawaii

### Same-Day Delivery
- **Cost**: $14.99
- **Delivery Time**: Same day (order by 12 PM local time)
- **Availability**: Select metro areas only (NYC, LA, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, Seattle)

## Delivery Times

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. Orders placed after 2 PM EST are processed the next business day.

### Processing Time
- In-stock items: 1-2 business days
- Personalized items: 3-5 business days
- Pre-order items: Ships on release date

### Holiday Shipping Deadlines

For guaranteed delivery by December 25th:
- Standard Shipping: Order by December 14th
- Express Shipping: Order by December 20th
- Next-Day Shipping: Order by December 23rd

## Order Tracking

### How to Track Your Order

1. Check your email for shipping confirmation with tracking number
2. Visit acmestore.com/track
3. Enter your order number and email address
4. View real-time tracking updates

You can also track orders by logging into your account and viewing Order History.

### Tracking Statuses Explained

- **Order Placed**: We've received your order
- **Processing**: Your order is being prepared
- **Shipped**: Your package is on the way
- **Out for Delivery**: Package will arrive today
- **Delivered**: Package has been delivered

## International Shipping

We ship to over 100 countries worldwide.

### International Rates
- **Standard International**: $19.99 (10-21 business days)
- **Express International**: $39.99 (5-10 business days)

### Customs & Duties

International orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, and customs fees. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping charges. Contact your local customs office for more information.

### Restricted Countries

Due to shipping restrictions, we cannot ship to: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, or Crimea region.

## Shipping Issues

### Package Not Received

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package:

1. Check around your property (porch, garage, with neighbors)
2. Wait 24 hours - sometimes tracking updates early
3. Contact your local carrier with the tracking number
4. If still not found after 48 hours, contact us for a replacement or refund

### Damaged Package

If your package arrives damaged:

1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and items
2. Contact us within 7 days of delivery
3. We'll send a replacement at no cost
4. No need to return the damaged item

### Wrong Address

If you entered the wrong shipping address:

- **Before shipping**: Contact us immediately to update
- **After shipping**: We cannot redirect packages in transit. If returned to us, we'll reship to the correct address (additional shipping may apply)

## Shipping Restrictions

The following items have shipping restrictions:

- **Hazardous materials**: Ground shipping only, no air transport
- **Oversized items**: Additional $20 surcharge, standard shipping only
- **Perishable items**: Express or Next-Day shipping required
- **Gift cards**: Email delivery only (instant)
